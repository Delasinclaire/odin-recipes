Pancakes, the best

Description

Golden discs of fluffy warm buttery airy cakes. Make this recipe and impress your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry Ingredients together
  2. Make a well then add wet ingredients, stir to combine
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes then flip
  5. Cook for another 2 minutes or until brown
Thats it, eat now
