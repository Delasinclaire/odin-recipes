Lou Ferrigno's Panini

Description

This is a hot pressed sandwich made with sandwich stuff, I mean it's alright ya know?

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Pesto sauce
  • Gabbagol
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil

Steps to make the sammich

  1. Grab your bread and spread some sauce on it
  2. Heat up that Panini press
  3. Spread that pesto all over the bread on both pieces
  4. Now layer the cheese and meat
  5. Close it up, and drizzle that olive oil on both sides of the sammy
  6. Put a little oil on the plancha
  7. plop that sandwich in the press
  8. Let it sizzle for like 5 minutes
  9. Take it out, let rest and enjoy
